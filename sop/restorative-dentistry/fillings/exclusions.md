NorthRiver does not reimburse for certain direct restoration procedures.

## Non-Covered Scenarios
*   **Cosmetic Replacement**: The cosmetic replacement of sound, undamaged amalgam fillings with composite purely for appearance is not covered. This is considered a non-covered cosmetic service (refer to Chapter 20).
*   **Polishing/Refinishing**: The polishing or refinishing of an existing restoration is considered part of routine maintenance and is not separately reimbursable.
*   **Re-treatment within 24 Months**: Where a filling is placed on the same tooth and surface that NorthRiver reimbursed within the previous twenty-four months, the later claim is treated as a re-treatment and is reviewed before payment. Durable restorations are not normally expected to fail within this period, warranting review.
