The eligible amount for a filling is determined by the number of tooth surfaces treated, not by the material used.

## Reimbursement Rules
*   **Single-surface restoration**: Simplest repair, carries a base eligible amount.
*   **Two, three or more surfaces**: Progressively more complex and carry a higher eligible amount.

## Examiner Responsibilities
Examiners must confirm that the number of surfaces billed is consistent with the identified tooth and the clinical notes. This verification is crucial as surface count is the most common source of inflated filling claims.
