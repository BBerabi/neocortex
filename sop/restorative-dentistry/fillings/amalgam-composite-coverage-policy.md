Amalgam and composite restorations on permanent and primary teeth are covered under all products.

## Conditions for Coverage
Coverage is provided when decay, fracture, or wear is documented.

## Eligible Amount Calculation
The eligible amount is set per restored tooth, based on the surface count, according to the applicable schedule.

## Multiple Restorations on the Same Tooth
Where two separate restorations are placed on the same tooth on the same date, they are normally treated as a single multi-surface restoration rather than two independent fillings.
