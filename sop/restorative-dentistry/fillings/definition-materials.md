A direct restoration, commonly called a filling, is a dental procedure that repairs a tooth in a single visit.

## Procedure
The material is placed directly into the prepared cavity.

## Covered Materials
NorthRiver reimburses two types of filling materials:

*   **Amalgam**: Primarily used on posterior (back) teeth.
*   **Composite Resin**: A tooth-coloured material suitable for both anterior (front) and posterior teeth.
