For all restorative procedures in Chapter 16 and subsequent chapters, the tooth number is considered a mandatory clinical field.

## Policy
Reimbursement for restorative procedures cannot normally be determined unless the specific tooth requiring repair is clearly identified throughout the claim. Examiners are required to treat the tooth number as a mandatory clinical field for every procedure.
