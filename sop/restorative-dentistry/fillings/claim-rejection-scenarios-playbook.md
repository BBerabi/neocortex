This playbook provides guidance for examiners on how to handle common scenarios that might lead to the rejection or suspension of direct restoration (filling) claims.

## Scenario 1: Surface Count Inconsistency
*   **Issue**: The surface count billed cannot be reconciled with the tooth (e.g., a four-surface restoration claimed on a lower incisor).
*   **Action**: The examiner should request clarification from the provider rather than declining the claim outright.

## Scenario 2: Early Failure of Previously Reimbursed Restoration
*   **Issue**: A previously reimbursed restoration on the same tooth and surface has failed within twenty-four months.
*   **Action**: Reimbursement of the replacement may be limited or declined depending on the documented cause of failure.
