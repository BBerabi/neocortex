To process a direct restoration (filling) claim, the following information is necessary:

## Mandatory Information
*   **Tooth Number**: Must identify the specific tooth treated.
*   **Surfaces Restored**: Clearly state the number of surfaces treated.
*   **Material Used**: Specify the filling material (e.g., amalgam, composite resin).
*   **CDT Procedure Code**: The correct Current Dental Terminology code.

## Additional Documentation Requirements
*   **Clinical Note for Re-treatment/Multi-surface**: For a re-treatment within twenty-four months or for any restoration of four or more surfaces, a brief clinical note describing the reason for treatment should accompany the claim.
*   **Radiograph**: A radiograph is not normally required for a routine single- or two-surface filling unless the claim is selected for quality review.
