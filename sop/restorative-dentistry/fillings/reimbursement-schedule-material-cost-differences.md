This section outlines the plan limitations, reimbursement percentages, and eligible amounts for direct restorations (fillings) across different NorthRiver products, as well as policies regarding material cost differences.

## Plan Limitations and Reimbursement Schedule

| Product | Reimbursement | Single surface | Two surfaces | Three or more surfaces |
|:--------|--------------:|---------------:|-------------:|-----------------------:|
| Smile Basic | 60% | €60 | €85 | €110 |
| Smile Plus | 70% | €70 | €100 | €130 |
| Smile Complete | 80% | €80 | €115 | €150 |
| Smile Elite | 80% | €90 | €130 | €170 |

## Composite on Posterior Teeth Reimbursement
*   **Smile Basic and Smile Plus**: Composite restorations on posterior teeth are reimbursed at the same eligible amount as the equivalent amalgam restoration. The difference in material cost is not reimbursed under these two products and is the member's responsibility.
*   **Smile Complete and Smile Elite**: These products reimburse composite on posterior teeth at the full composite eligible amount shown above.
