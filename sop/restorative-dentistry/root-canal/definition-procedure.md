Endodontic treatment, commonly known as a root canal, is a dental procedure designed to save a tooth that would otherwise require extraction.

## Procedure
It involves:
1.  Removing infected or inflamed tissue from inside the tooth.
2.  Cleaning and shaping the internal canals.
3.  Sealing the canals to prevent further infection.

## Eligible Amount
The eligible amount for a root canal depends on the number of canals treated, which varies by tooth type:
*   **Front teeth**: Typically have one canal.
*   **Premolars**: Typically have one or two canals.
*   **Molars**: Typically have three or more canals.
