NorthRiver does not cover certain endodontic treatment (root canal) procedures.

## Non-Covered Scenarios
*   **Non-Restorable Tooth**: Root canal treatment on a tooth that is not restorable (i.e., a tooth that will require extraction regardless) is not covered, as the procedure offers no lasting benefit.
*   **Smile Basic Product**: Routine root canal treatment is not covered under the Smile Basic product.
*   **Elective Re-treatment within 12 Months**: Elective re-treatment within twelve months of the original procedure is reviewed before payment, implying potential non-coverage if not clinically justified.
