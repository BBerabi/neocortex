This section outlines the plan limitations, reimbursement percentages, and eligible amounts for endodontic treatment (root canal) across different NorthRiver products, and clarifies rules for combined treatments.

## Plan Limitations and Reimbursement Schedule

| Product | Reimbursement | Single canal | Two canals | Three or more canals |
|:--------|--------------:|-------------:|-----------:|---------------------:|
| Smile Basic | Not covered | — | — | — |
| Smile Plus | 60% | €280 | €380 | €520 |
| Smile Complete | 70% | €320 | €430 | €580 |
| Smile Elite | 80% | €360 | €480 | €640 |

## Combined Root Canal and Crown Treatment
Where a root canal and a crown are performed on the same tooth, each is reimbursed separately under its own rules.

## Annual Benefit Limit
The annual benefit limit continues to apply to the combined total cost of both procedures. A member with a limited remaining annual benefit may have part of the combined treatment declined once the annual maximum is reached.
