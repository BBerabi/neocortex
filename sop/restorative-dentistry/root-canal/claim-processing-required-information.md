To process an endodontic treatment (root canal) claim, the following information is necessary:

## Mandatory Information
*   **Tooth Identification**: The claim must identify the specific tooth treated.
*   **Number of Canals**: Specify the number of canals treated.
*   **Procedure Code**: The relevant CDT procedure code.

## Additional Documentation Requirements
*   **Pre-operative Radiograph**: A pre-operative radiograph is required for all root canal claims.
*   **Post-operative Radiograph**: A post-operative radiograph is expected for molar treatment.
*   **For Re-treatment Claims**: The documentation must establish that the original treatment failed and that the tooth remains restorable.
