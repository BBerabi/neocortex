Root canal treatment is covered when the tooth is restorable and worth retaining, supported by a radiograph and clinical notes.

## Covered Scenarios
*   **Initial Treatment**: For teeth that are restorable and suitable for retention, with proper documentation.
*   **Re-treatment**: Re-treatment of a previously root-filled tooth is covered if the original treatment has failed and the tooth remains restorable.

## Eligible Amount
The eligible amount for root canal treatment increases with the number of canals treated.
