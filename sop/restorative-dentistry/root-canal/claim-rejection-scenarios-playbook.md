This playbook provides guidance for examiners on how to handle common scenarios that might lead to the rejection or suspension of endodontic treatment (root canal) claims.

## Scenario 1: Missing Radiograph
*   **Issue**: A root canal claim is submitted without a required radiograph.
*   **Action**: The claim should be suspended for documentation.

## Scenario 2: Non-Restorable Tooth
*   **Issue**: The documentation indicates that the tooth was subsequently extracted, or was not restorable at the time of root canal treatment.
*   **Action**: Reimbursement for the root canal is declined. The member should be directed to the extraction and tooth-replacement benefits instead.
