A crown is a custom-made restoration that covers the entire visible portion of a tooth.

## Purpose
It is used when significant tooth structure has been lost to the extent that a filling would not be durable. Common scenarios include:
*   After a root canal
*   After a significant fracture
*   Where extensive decay has undermined the tooth

## Treatment Process
Crowns are fabricated outside the mouth and fitted at a later appointment. Therefore, the treatment usually spans two visits. The claim may be submitted either on the preparation date or the fitting date, depending on the provider's billing practice.
