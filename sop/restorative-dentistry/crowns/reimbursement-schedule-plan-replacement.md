This section outlines the plan limitations, reimbursement percentages, maximum eligible amounts, and replacement intervals for dental crowns across different NorthRiver products.

## Plan Limitations and Reimbursement Schedule

| Product | Reimbursement | Maximum eligible amount | Replacement interval |
|:--------|--------------:|------------------------:|---------------------:|
| Smile Basic | Not covered | — | — |
| Smile Plus | 50% | €600 | One per tooth every 7 years |
| Smile Complete | 60% | €750 | One per tooth every 5 years |
| Smile Elite | 70% | €900 | One per tooth every 5 years |

## Replacement Interval Policy
The replacement interval commences from the date NorthRiver last reimbursed a crown on the same tooth. A replacement claimed before this interval has elapsed will be reviewed against the documented reason for failure. Early replacement due to recurrent decay or fracture may be reimbursed, while replacement for cosmetic reasons will be declined.
