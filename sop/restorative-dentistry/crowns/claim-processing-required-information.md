To process a dental crown claim, the following information is necessary:

## Mandatory Information
*   **Tooth Identification**: The claim must identify the specific tooth treated.
*   **Crown Material**: Specify the material used for the crown.
*   **Procedure Code**: The relevant CDT procedure code.

## Additional Documentation Requirements
*   **Clinical Notes for Necessity**: Clinical notes establishing *why* a crown was necessary instead of a filling are required. These notes should support the extent of tooth destruction.
*   **Pre-operative Radiograph**: A pre-operative radiograph showing the extent of tooth destruction must be submitted.
*   **For Replacement Crowns**: The documentation must state the reason the previous crown failed and its approximate placement date.
