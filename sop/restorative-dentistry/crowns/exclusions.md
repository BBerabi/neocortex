NorthRiver does not cover certain dental crown procedures.

## Non-Covered Scenarios
*   **Purely Cosmetic Reasons**: Crowns placed for purely cosmetic reasons on sound teeth are not covered (refer to Chapter 20).
*   **Replacement within Interval**: The replacement of an existing crown within the applicable replacement interval is not covered, *unless* the existing crown has failed for a documented clinical reason such as recurrent decay, fracture, or loss of retention.
*   **Smile Basic Product**: Crowns are not covered under the Smile Basic product, which is designed for preventive and basic restorative care only.
