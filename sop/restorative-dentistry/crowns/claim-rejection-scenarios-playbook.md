This playbook provides guidance for examiners on how to handle common scenarios that might lead to the rejection or modification of dental crown claims.

## Scenario 1: Missing Documentation
*   **Issue**: A crown claim is submitted without a supporting radiograph or without clinical notes establishing the loss of tooth structure.
*   **Action**: The claim should be suspended for documentation.

## Scenario 2: Crown on a Potentially Fillable Tooth
*   **Issue**: A crown is claimed for a tooth that could reasonably have been restored with a filling, and the documentation does not adequately establish the need for a crown.
*   **Action**: Reimbursement may be limited to the eligible amount for a multi-surface filling, with the difference in cost declined.
