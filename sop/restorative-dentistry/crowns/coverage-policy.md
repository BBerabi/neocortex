NorthRiver reimburses for a crown when the tooth cannot be adequately restored by a direct filling, provided this necessity is supported by clinical documentation.

## Covered Materials
Covered materials for crowns include:
*   Metal
*   Porcelain-fused-to-metal
*   All-ceramic crowns

## Eligible Amount
The eligible amount for a crown is the same regardless of the material chosen. If a member selects a more expensive material than the eligible amount covers, the difference in cost is the member's responsibility.
