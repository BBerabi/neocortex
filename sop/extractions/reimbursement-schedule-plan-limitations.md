This section outlines the plan limitations, eligible amounts, and reimbursement percentages for simple, surgical, and impacted tooth extractions across different NorthRiver products.

## Plan Limitations and Reimbursement Schedule

| Product | Simple extraction | Surgical extraction | Impacted tooth |
|:--------|------------------:|--------------------:|---------------:|
| Smile Basic | €70 (100%) | Covered only if infection or pain documented | Not covered |
| Smile Plus | €75 (100%) | €160 (80%) | €210 (80%) |
| Smile Complete | €85 (100%) | €200 (90%) | €260 (90%) |
| Smile Elite | €95 (100%) | €240 (100%) | €320 (100%) |

## Reimbursement Policy
*   **Simple Extractions**: Reimbursed at the full eligible amount across all products, due to their inexpensive and unambiguous nature.
*   **Surgical and Impacted Extractions**: Subject to the product's specific reimbursement percentage as shown in the table.
