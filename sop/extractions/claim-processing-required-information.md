To process a tooth extraction claim, the following information is necessary:

## Mandatory Information
*   **Tooth Identification**: The claim must identify each tooth removed.
*   **Type of Extraction**: Specify whether it was a simple, surgical, or impacted extraction.

## Additional Documentation Requirements
*   **Clinical Notes**: For any surgical or impacted extraction, clinical notes describing the indication (reason for treatment) are required.
*   **Pre-operative Radiograph**: A pre-operative radiograph should be submitted for any surgical or impacted extraction.
*   **Multiple Extractions Itemization**: Where multiple teeth are removed in one appointment, each tooth must be listed separately with its own procedure code. A single combined charge covering several teeth without itemization is insufficient.
