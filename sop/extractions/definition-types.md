An extraction is the removal of a tooth that cannot be saved or that must be removed for orthodontic or prosthetic reasons.

## Types of Extractions
NorthRiver distinguishes between two main types of extractions:

*   **Simple Extraction**: Performed on a fully erupted tooth, typically using forceps.
*   **Surgical Extraction**: Requires incision of the gum, removal of bone, or sectioning of the tooth. This type includes the removal of impacted wisdom teeth.

These two types are reimbursed at different eligible amounts and require distinct documentation.
