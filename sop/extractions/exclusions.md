NorthRiver does not cover certain tooth extraction procedures.

## Non-Covered Scenarios
*   **Asymptomatic Wisdom Teeth**: The removal of asymptomatic, fully erupted wisdom teeth purely as a precautionary measure is not covered unless accompanied by documentation of recurrent infection, decay, or damage to an adjacent tooth.
*   **Extractions for Non-Covered Plans**: Extractions performed solely to facilitate a non-covered cosmetic or orthodontic plan will follow the coverage status of that overall plan, meaning they will not be covered.
