This playbook provides guidance for examiners on how to handle common scenarios that might lead to the rejection or suspension of tooth extraction claims.

## Scenario 1: Surgical Extraction Missing Documentation
*   **Issue**: A surgical extraction is billed without a supporting radiograph or clinical note.
*   **Action**: The claim should be suspended for documentation rather than declined outright.

## Scenario 2: Asymptomatic Wisdom Tooth Removal Without Indication
*   **Issue**: Removal of an asymptomatic wisdom tooth is claimed without supporting documentation.
*   **Action**: The claim is declined under all products, unless the member's product and clinical notes explicitly establish a covered indication (e.g., recurrent infection, damage to adjacent tooth).
