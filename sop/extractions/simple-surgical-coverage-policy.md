## Simple Extractions
Simple extractions are covered under all products where the clinical reason is documented. Examples of documented clinical reasons include:
*   Advanced decay
*   Non-restorable fracture
*   Severe periodontal disease

## Surgical Extractions
Surgical extractions, including the removal of impacted wisdom teeth, are covered under Smile Plus, Smile Complete, and Smile Elite.

*   **Smile Basic**: Covers surgical extraction *only* where the procedure is necessary to treat infection or pain, rather than performed electively.
