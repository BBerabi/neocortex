NorthRiver has specific policies regarding the replacement of existing fixed bridges and removable dentures.

## Non-Covered Scenarios
*   **Replacement Before Interval**: The replacement of a functioning bridge or denture before its established replacement interval has elapsed is generally not covered.
*   **Exception**: Early replacement *is* covered if the existing prosthesis can no longer be made serviceable due to a documented reason (e.g., supporting teeth have since been lost, or the prosthesis itself is irrevocably damaged).

## Routine Maintenance
Routine relines and adjustments of an existing denture within six months of its original fitting are considered part of the initial treatment and are not separately reimbursable.
