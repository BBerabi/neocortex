This section details the plan limitations, reimbursement percentages, eligible amounts, and replacement intervals for prosthodontic treatments, including complete dentures, partial dentures, and bridges, across different NorthRiver products. It also addresses the application of the annual benefit limit.

## Plan Limitations and Reimbursement Schedule

| Product | Reimbursement | Complete denture (per arch) | Partial denture (per arch) | Bridge (per unit) | Replacement interval |
|:--------|--------------:|----------------------------:|---------------------------:|------------------:|---------------------:|
| Smile Basic | Not covered | — | — | — | — |
| Smile Plus | 50% | €700 | €600 | €350 | 7 years |
| Smile Complete | 50% | €900 | €750 | €450 | 7 years |
| Smile Elite | 60% | €1,100 | €900 | €550 | 5 years |

## Annual Benefit Limit Application
Prosthodontic reimbursement is subject to the annual benefit limit, similar to all other treatments. Given that multi-unit bridges or full dentures often exceed a member's remaining annual balance, examiners should typically reimburse up to the unused portion of the annual maximum and decline the remainder with an explanation, rather than rejecting the claim entirely.
