## Fixed Bridge
A fixed bridge replaces one or more missing teeth using artificial teeth anchored to crowns placed on the natural teeth on either side of the gap.

## Removable Denture
A removable denture replaces missing teeth using a prosthesis that rests on the gums. In the case of a partial denture, it also clasps onto the remaining teeth.

## Choice of Prosthesis
The choice between a fixed bridge and a removable denture depends on the number and position of the missing teeth, as well as the condition of the remaining teeth. NorthRiver reimburses whichever approach is clinically appropriate, subject to specific plan limitations.
