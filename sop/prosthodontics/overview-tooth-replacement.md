Prosthodontic treatment focuses on replacing teeth that have already been lost. NorthRiver covers two primary approaches for tooth replacement under this chapter: fixed bridges and removable dentures.

## Key Characteristics
*   **Fixed Bridges**: Permanently cemented onto adjacent teeth.
*   **Removable Dentures**: Prostheses that members can take out, resting on the gums, and for partial dentures, clasping onto remaining teeth.
*   **Dental Implants**: Addressed separately in Chapter 20 due to differing coverage status.

## Cost and Reimbursement
Prosthodontic treatment is the most expensive category covered by NorthRiver and is reimbursed at the lowest percentages. It is not available under Smile Basic.

## Claim Assessment
Due to the high cost, every prosthodontic claim requires careful assessment against the member's remaining annual benefit limit. Many such claims will fall within the senior-review threshold, necessitating thorough review.
