NorthRiver reimburses for fixed bridges and removable dentures to replace teeth under specific conditions.

## Covered Scenarios
*   **Teeth Lost During Cover**: Replacement of teeth that were lost while the member held active coverage.
*   **Teeth Lost Before Joining**: Replacement of teeth that the member can document were lost before joining NorthRiver, subject to the 'missing-tooth provision' (refer to relevant policy).

## Types of Dentures
*   **Complete Denture**: Replaces a full arch of teeth.
*   **Partial Denture**: Replaces several teeth within an arch.

## Bridge Eligible Amount
The eligible amount for a bridge depends on the number of units. Each artificial tooth and each supporting crown counts as one unit.
